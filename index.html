Riding Lessons

There are various riding centres throughout the Uk. Some have riding instructors that have completed up to BHS Stage 4 qualifications, others have advanced to a coach specifying in certain disciplines such as dressage, show jumping or cross country. While there are variations in the level of instructor qualifications, some like to specialise in teaching complete beginners, some focus on intermediate and experienced while others like to only teach jumping classes.

Finding the right Riding Stables for you is important both for travel costs, lesson expense and ensuring that you get the most from an instructor during your lessons. Use our below map to browse locations throughout the UK and hopefully you'll find a riding Stables nearest to your doorstep!

Treking, Hacking and Pub Rides

While lessons are a great way to start learning to ride, you may prefer a relaxing experience in the countryside, giving you the opportunity to explore and visit new locations while taking in the sun and fresh air. Riding stables can also provide half day or full day rides for beginners or faster paced hacks and pub riders for those that have many years of riding experience.

Our below map also lists areas that provide treks, hacks, pub rides, riding holidays and pony days. Zoom in on an area you'd love to visit and browse the available riding stables.
